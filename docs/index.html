Thea

A tiny and fast view library in JS.

Thea just has one concern: the view. State is your concern.

Views should be tiny and fast. Declared, not defined. Not hidden behind a big machine, but there to grab if you need them. Rendered immediately, unless you choose otherwise. No classes, just objects. No framework, just functions. Thea hopes to enable just that.

It’s early days though. If you’re curious, check out the repository. Or better yet, dive into some examples:

  • The classical todo app, redone.
  • A annoyingly addictive little game

They’re both also live: just follow the link from their readme.
